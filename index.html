Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Recall ("the app") is designed to keep your data private and on your device. This policy explains what data we handle and how.

1. Data stored on your device

The app stores the following data only on your device (we do not upload your saved items, PIN, or preferences to our servers):

  • Your saves — Item names, details, categories, and any photos you add.
  • App preferences — Sort order, theme (light/dark), first-launch and "never show again" choices.
  • App lock — If you enable app lock, a secure representation of your PIN is stored on device only. Biometric use is handled by your device’s OS.

This data stays in the app’s local storage and is not sent to our servers.

2. No account required

You can use Recall without creating an account. We do not collect your name, email, or other account information for the core app experience.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app may show ads (banner and rewarded) provided by Google AdMob. When ads are shown:

  • Google may collect data (e.g. device identifiers, coarse location) for ad delivery and measurement, as described in Google’s Privacy Policy.
  • If you purchase Recall Pro, ads can be disabled and this ad-related data collection may no longer apply for you.

4. In-app purchases

Pro upgrades are processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment details; only the store’s standard purchase state (e.g. that you own Pro) is used by the app.

5. Photos

If you add photos to items, they are stored on your device as part of the app’s local data. We do not upload or analyze the content of your photos.

6. Data security

Your data is stored in the app’s private storage on your device. You can further protect access using the in-app lock (PIN or biometrics). We do not transmit your saves or PIN to our servers.

7. Children

Recall is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will be revised when we do. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or the app’s data practices, please contact us at the support address provided in the app or on the store listing.
